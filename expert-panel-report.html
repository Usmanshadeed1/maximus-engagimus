Expert Panel Recommendations for Digital Marketing Automation

What Exists Today

The tool is currently built around one core workflow: social media engagement via AI-generated comments. Everything else supports that mission. You have a solid foundation — now the panel recommends strategic expansions to automate your entire operation.

✓ Already Built

  • Client profiles (voice, keywords, sample comments, industry sites, target audience)
  • AI comment generation with multi-provider fallback (8 providers)
  • Platform-specific prompts (10 platforms)
  • Content analysis & keyword-to-client matching
  • Competitor tracking & sighting logs
  • Comment history with basic stats
  • AI provider config & "No API" clipboard mode
  • Org/team management with RLS & audit logs

✗ Not Yet Built (The Gaps)

  • No SEO tooling whatsoever
  • No content creation beyond comments
  • No scheduling or content calendar
  • No batch/queue processing
  • No exportable client reports
  • No off-page engagement (forums, reviews, directories)
  • No email or ad copy generation
  • No command palette or bulk actions

The Situation

You manage 20+ clients simultaneously. Your team does ALL of these manually: content writing, SEO work, media production, and reporting. The panel was asked: "What can we automate or semi-automate to save the most human hours while leveraging the AI infrastructure that's already there?"

Everything below builds on your existing database schema, AI pipeline, and prompt system. No reinventing the wheel.
